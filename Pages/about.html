About BCAD

Who is BCAD?

BCAD is an organisation established on 31st July 2017 to integrate and empower people by God's word who are residing in Denmark. The name is based on Bhutanese as it is established by Bhutanese people living in Denmark. The organisation is not only concerned with the Bhutanese but integrate all the people who are inneed of help, love, affection and intregation.

The association is based on a Christian values and works in connection with locals churches, associations and companies and coordinate the work with them. The association collaborates with KIT - Kirkernes Integrations Tjeneste.

Vision

Build and improve spiritual relation among people in Denmark by sharing love and affection.

Mission

Help and encourage people to know God's words. Bring different types of programs to understand life through the love of the God.

Purpose

  • Meet and do Fellowships
  • Build and keep network among us by bringing God's words
  • Help and guide to follow Christ ways of living
  • Inspire people to share love and blessing
